Orderd List

  1. Purpose:It is used for creating list with items
  2. Default:Items Are Automatically Numbered
  3. Nesting:Nesting of list is possible
>

Examples

  1. laptop
    1. Dell
    2. HP
    3. Lenove
  2. Mobiles:
    1. Samsung
    2. Apple
    3. Google
  1. Numeric: Default Type(1,2,3)
    Attribute:type="1"

  2. Uppercase Letters: (A,B,C)
    Attribute:type="A"

  3. Lowercase Letters: (a,b,c)
    Attribute:type="a"

  4. Roman Letters: (I,II,III)
    Attribute:type="I"

  5. Lowercase Roman Letters: (A,B,C)
    Attribute:type="i"
